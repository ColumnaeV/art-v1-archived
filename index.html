The Featured Exhibit

"Pure Urban Colours" is an exhibit that displays my current beginner work into the application of Adobe Photoshop. In this collection of digitally edited images, I mainly utilised double exposure and styled brush techniques. I would also would like to thank Jeff Zhao, Austin Nguyen, Ethan McDermott, Evelyn Wan, Pratik Chaduvula, Vincie Wong, Alex Suh, Leily Zhu, Abhinav Jaddu, Naman Nisheeth, and Owen Gong as various contributors to this collection. More specifically, their efforts in photography, modeling, and editing advice is what makes "Pure Urban Colours" truly vibrant in colour.

Don't forget to check here every so often (or on my social media) for new releases of exhibits!

The Artist

Hello there! My name is Christian and I’m the developer of this site. I’ve recently left Millard North High School whilst graduating with my regular and IB diplomas in search of higher education. Currently, I’m attending university in the state of California as a first year to major in Medicinal Chemistry and Computer Science.

I am an experienced free-lance web developer and graphic designer previously from Nebraska. Always caring for others to succeed in life, I co-founded C&C Studios in Southern California, with my cousin and one of my best mates, to help those who are in need of a platform for artistic exposure. Within the last couple of years, I have been developing an expertise in graphic design whilst advancing my artistic ability in frontend design. Atypical to my current technological repertoire, I plan on pursuing my humanitarian ideals by providing for those who are in need of immediate care through my future studies in the field of medicine.

Diverging from code based art, I decided to try out digital artwork. This website displays only my own personalised work from edits to unedited photography. I hope you enjoy it!
